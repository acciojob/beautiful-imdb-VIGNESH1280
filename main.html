Simran, a traditional and obedient girl, dreams of her ideal love, but her life takes a turn when her strict father, Baldev Singh (played by Amrish Puri), arranges her marriage to his friend's son in Punjab, India. Before the marriage, Simran convinces her father to allow her to go on a month-long trip across Europe with her friends. During the trip, she meets Raj, a carefree and fun-loving young man. Initially, they don't get along, but as they spend more time together, they start to fall in love.
